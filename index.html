Vision Quest

Bringing a Most Important Question To Mother Earth

In the shade of this big oak, you can enter the realm of deep listening - to yourself, to the land and to Life herself.

If you find yourself in need of quiet and peace, consider gifting yourself with six full and spacious days to allow the quieter voices of your soul to speak to you.

Somewhere between this moment and eternity, there is a life to be led. How do we approach our life now? What is the central question that your life is revolving around these days? Exploring these questions in deep relationship with our Mother Earth is one way to describe a Vision Quest.
